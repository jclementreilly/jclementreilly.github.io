Beautiful Exposure

Advertising, Digital

A Sun Screen Awareness Campaign

Challenge: There is a vast amount of information available about the prevalence of skin cancer; yet, Americans continue to exposure themselves to ultraviolet light.

Solution: An Android and iOS application that teaches users the dangers about overexposure to UV light on one's skin and teaches users how sunscreen counteracts this damage

Target Audience: 15 to 25 year old women who are tanning bed users and sun worships. They never use sunscreen or never reapply sunscreen throughout the day.

Contribution: Research, Prototyping, User Testing, Interface and Experience Design, Photographing, Branding


Inspiration

I wear sunscreen everyday because I know that it protects our skin from UV light. To prove this theory, I studied the science behind ultraviolet light photography. The reason why my face is turning black in this video is because the sunscreen is absorbing the ultraviolet light in my location, thus visually proving that sunscreen does protect our skin. This inspired a whole project dedicated to teaching others about how to protect their skin.
