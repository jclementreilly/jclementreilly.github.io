Ami Vitale Photo Book

Digital

A Timeless Photo Book

Challenge: Create a photo book for world renowned photographer, Ami Vitale. Pair with a photography student who will photo sequence the images to tell a story.

Solution: A iPad photo book with a constrained design. Ami's images are timeless and meant to tell a story about the complicated relationship between humans and nature. The design needed to let the photos speak for themselves, literally. Users can listen to Ami talk about her photographs.

Target Audience: Anyone Ami chooses to share her story with

Consideration: Via a Skype call, Ami said she wanted a digital piece that she could share with others besides her website, so we created a digital book instead of a traditional printed one.

Contribution: Research, Prototyping, User Testing, Interface and Experience Design, Team Collaboration
