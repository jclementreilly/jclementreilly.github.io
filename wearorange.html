Wear Orange

Campaign, Digital, Print

A Gun-Violence Awareness Campaign

Challenge: Among many Americans, the topic of gun violence sparks thoughts of political views and polarization. So much so that many whom were interviewed diverted away from the conversation to ignore a political argument.

Solution: A campaign that switches the narrative around gun violence from politics to human life and safety

Target Audience: Parents who are afraid to send their children to school, Millennial Activists who want more for their generation, and lawmakers who promise to uphold the human right to life, liberty and the pursuit of happiness

Contribution: Research, Concept Creation, Print and Interface Design, Branding
