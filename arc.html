Arc of Onondaga

Campaign, Digital

A Social Impact Campaign

Challenge: How will Arc compete with local agencies who have similar goals as Arc of Onondaga: to assist individuals with developmental disabilities achieve their fullest potential?

Solution: A full marketing campaign, involving print and digital deliverables, that have a more modern in look and feel

Target Audience: Families of 18 year olds who are looking for services once their children leave the school system

Consideration: This project was a collaboration between student photographers and designers. Designer, Victoria Amoroso, was responsible for the print design.

Contribution Research, Prototyping, User Testing, Interface Design, Team Collaboration
