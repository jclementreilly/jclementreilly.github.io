About Me

Hi there! I am a recent graduate of the Newhouse School of Public Communications at Syracuse University. With a graphic design and psychology degree, I am pursuing a career that will allow me to use my creative passion to make a difference. Whether that be in an advertising agency, a small design boutique or a Fortune 500 company, I only want to create with purpose. When I am not busy designing, I am on the beach in Cape Cod with my family, going for a run, wearing Orange proudly in the Dome, or on the pursuit of my next Instagram.

Remembrance Scholarship

One of the greatest honors I have ever been a part of is the Remembrance Program at Syracuse University. In 1988, thirty-five Syracuse students out of 270 were killed in the bombing of Pan Am Flight 103. Each year, 35 Remembrance Scholarships are awarded to senior undergraduate students on the basis of distinguished academic achievement, citizenship, and service to community. Scholars organize events for Remembrance Week in October and carry on the legacy of those who lost their lives. I look back and act forward in memory of Julianne F. Kelly, a woman whom I hope to one day name my daughter after.
