About Me

My name is Jacqueline, but my friends call me Jackie. You can too.

Much to my mother's surprise, my childhood mascara paintings on her bathroom sink and the green crayon designs on the wall were a sign of great things to come — I am a natural born creative. After receiving my Bachelor of Science from the Newhouse School of Communications at Syracuse University, I began my career with IBM Design. Combining my passion for art, communication and psychology, I put users at the forefront of everything I create.
