A Stationery Internship

Hello Little One is a design studio committed to creating family stationery, photo gifts, and wedding invitations. HLO partners with Shutterfly, Tiny Prints, and Wedding Paper Divas.

Challenge: How do you differentiate the Hello Little One brand from other brands? How does fine art translate into a digital medium and how doesn't it?

Solution: Unique, hand-crafted and digitally-manipulated invitations and stationery creations for every occasion

Consideration: My background in drawing and paint helped out in this internship experience.

Target Audience: 30 to 50 year old women looking for a high quality but cost-conscious stationery system to celebrate an occasion in their lives

Contribution: All designs were a collaboration between my boss, Jenny Romanski, and me. I was mostly responsible for all the fine art contributions.

Flower Border Save The Date
