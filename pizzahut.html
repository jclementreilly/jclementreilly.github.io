Pizza Hut

Advertising

A Campaign that is Fast

Challenge: Create an advertising campaign aimed at increasing Pizza Hut's brand recognition and driving the number of customers who use digital ordering.

Solution: A campaign centralized around the idea of "Feeding The Crave" — when you're hungry, you want food and you want it fast. Pizza Hut pizza, through the new, fast digital app, delivers pizza that satisfies even the biggest crave.

Target Audience: Millennials who satisfy their crave not by cooking their own food but by ordering out

Consideration: This project was a collaboration between Newhouse advertising, photo and design students.

Contribution: I was responsible for the magazine spreads and the newspaper advertisement. I collaborated with designer,  Lyndsey Jimenez,  who designed the iPhone application.
